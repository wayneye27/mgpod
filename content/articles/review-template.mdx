# Tool Review Template

## Quick Verdict

State who the tool is best for, who should skip it, and the main buying reason.

## Best For

- Seller type
- Store stage
- Workflow problem

## Core Features

Explain only features that affect the buyer decision. Avoid copying vendor marketing copy.

## Pros and Cons

Use specific tradeoffs. Do not invent first-hand claims unless you have tested the product.

## Pricing

Summarize the pricing model, then tell readers to verify current pricing on the official site.

## Alternatives

Mention at least two alternatives and the scenario where each one is better.

## FAQ

Answer buyer-intent questions based on real search queries and customer objections.
